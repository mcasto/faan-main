FAAN's Guide to Planned Giving
  1. Complete FAAN's Legacy Donation and Planned Giving Intention forms
  2. Determine the type of gift you wish to make to FAAN (outright bequest of a specific dollar amount, specific assets, or percentage of estate)
  3. Check with both your Ecuadorian and US attorney (if applicable) depending on asset location for correct legal language
  4. Include FAAN (Fundación Familia Amor Animal) in your final will and testament
