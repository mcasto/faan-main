FAAN's Current Shelter Project & Goals
Keeping Animals Safe in Cuenca, Ecuador

We did it! We raised funds to purchase permanent land for the dogs of FAAN who were facing eviction. We are now entering building construction so that our dogs will have a safe and modern sanctuary that, hand in hand with education, prevention, and adoption, will make a real difference in Cuenca's care and kindness for abandoned, rescued, and vulnerable dogs.

Project Goals:

  • Phase 1:
    • Purchase the land
      • Secure permits and create road access
      • Complete soil test to determine building location
      • Identify architect for detailed plan and specifications
      • Launch Capital Campaign to build the shelter
    • Create the needed infrastructure for building the new dog shelter (where we are now)
  • Phase 2:
    • Construction of the basic shelter structure
    • Moving our loving animals, particularly those at our upper shelter, who are now at imminent risk due to recent landslides at their temporary location
  • Phase 3:
    • Construction of the outer buildings
    • Completion of the shelter, including our adoption center, special areas for visiting vets/spay and neutering, grooming area for the dogs, and a community education room

Become part of our community vision to make Cuenca known as the "City That Cares" about Animal Welfare through your donations, volunteering, and provision of supplies for the construction of the new shelter.
