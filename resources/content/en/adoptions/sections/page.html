Adopt and Rescue

At the FAAN Foundation, we have many dogs dreaming of their Fur-ever homes. They range from adorable puppies to our senior dogs. We'll arrange for an adoption specialist to work with you and help you find your perfect match.

When you adopt from FAAN, your pet owner's benefits include:
  • VIP attention to your match with a FAAN adoption specialist
  • Arrangement of a meet and greet
  • Vaccines, fleas, and deworming at half price
  • Discounts on grooming and dog training accessories
  • Up to 30% of costs covered by FAAN in emergencies
You may also volunteer as a Foster Parent to help us manage emergency placements
