Maisey
I am a six-year-old, medium-sized dog whose former owner found me abandoned by my mother. At my old home, I was cared for very well and grew up to be a really sweet and friendly dog, but, sadly, my owner had to return to the United States and couldn't take me with them. My breed is a unique mixture of Poodle and Terrier, but I also have Border Collie-like features. Since I have been growing up and living with other dogs all my life, I am social and get along well with both kids and other animals. Currently I am living with my best friend, Sophie, with whom I have a strong bond. I'm a great companion to her, even though I am the more shy one of the two of us. Both of us being adopted to the same home isn't required, but would sure help us settle into our new home and get accustomed faster. I am very calm and loving and will fit into any family. I am also spayed, vaccinated, and housebroken. This is a wonderful adoption for an instant companion and loving, best friend for the family, particularly if you are not looking forward to training a puppy. If you live within 4 hours of Cuenca, we will bring Sophie and/or Maisey to you as part of FAAN's adoption support for these two special girls.
