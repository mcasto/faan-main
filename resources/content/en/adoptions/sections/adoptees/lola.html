Lola
I am a young, female dog and live with my mother, Ambar, in the shelter. I am very shy and need to be approached slowly and with caution since fast movements scare me. Sometimes my curious side comes out, and I'll enjoy your attention then. I also like mingling and making friends with other dogs. That's why, sometimes, you can find me in a different cuarto of the shelter. Normally I stay behind the more confident dogs, but when I'm alone with you, and you've earned my trust, you can count on me as a loyal and friendly companion who gets along well with other dogs.
