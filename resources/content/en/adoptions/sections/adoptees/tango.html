Tango
I am an experienced dog and almost resemble a lion. My name comes from my old days of passion and fire. Despite my age, I still enjoy going for walks and am well-behaved, although I also have nothing against a few breaks. I get along well with dogs my age, but the energy of younger dogs sometimes annoys me, and I won't hesitate to put them in their place. With humans, I am friendly and enjoy pets, but I also appreciate alone time to rest. My ideal owner would be relaxed and calm.
