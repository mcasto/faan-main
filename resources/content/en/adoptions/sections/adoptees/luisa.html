Luisa
I am a calm lady, about seven years old, who had a rough past. I used to fear people a lot, and still don't like a leash, but have started to trust a little more. I am very docile with other dogs and enjoy their company. If the other dogs are with me, I will come up to you and let you pet me even if I don't know you yet. I am anxious of brooms, which might be because I was beaten with them before being rescued. My owner will have to be patient in order to give me time to trust them, but as soon as I get used to my new surroundings and feel comfortable, I would also make a great family dog, since I am very patient and loving.
