We know how to PAWTY!

If you're an animal lover, save this page to meet up with and connect with Pawsome volunteers and donors who are working to create Ecuador's most modern and sustainable animal shelter.

We have an amazing community and educational programs and events focused on improving the lives of abused, abandoned, and senior dogs in the Cuenca, Ecuador metropolitan area. The bonus is that you will meet fellow animal lovers who are giving their time, talent, and treasure to improve the lives of the animals in our care.
