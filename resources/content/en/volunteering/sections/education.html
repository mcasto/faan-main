FAAN Focuses on Animal Welfare through Education

FAAN volunteers have begun the work on developing an education program and curriculum on animal welfare in Ecuador. The program will target early grade education as well as key point of influence in animal welfare within communities including veterinarians, pet stores and youth groups.

By developing young people’s knowledge about animals, we can inspire the next generation and create a future that’s kinder to animals.

91% of adults polled said young people should be taught kindness to animals at school.
